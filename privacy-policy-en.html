Privacy Policy

Basic Policy

Maruyama Pharmacy (hereinafter "our pharmacy") has established this privacy policy regarding the protection of patients' personal information, and strives to handle and protect such information appropriately.

Personal Information Collected

Our pharmacy collects the following information through the questionnaire:

  • Name, phone number
  • Health status (pregnancy, breastfeeding, etc.)
  • Allergies and adverse reaction history
  • Current medications and supplements
  • Medical history and lifestyle habits
  • Generic medicine preferences
  • Other information for the pharmacist

Purpose of Use

The collected personal information will be used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing appropriate medication counseling by pharmacists
  • Confirming medication safety (allergies, drug interactions, etc.)
  • Providing appropriate medical services to patients
  • Reporting to administrative agencies as required by law

If the information is to be used for purposes other than those listed above, we will obtain the patient's prior consent.

Information Transmission and Management

Transmission Method

Information entered in the questionnaire is securely transmitted to our pharmacy's system via Google Forms service. Communications are protected by SSL/TLS encryption.

Storage and Management

Collected personal information is managed in systems with appropriate security measures, and we strive to prevent unauthorized access, leakage, and loss.

Retention Period

Personal information is retained for the period prescribed by relevant laws, including the Pharmacists Act and the Pharmaceutical and Medical Devices Act. After the retention period, it is promptly deleted using secure methods.

Disclosure to Third Parties

Our pharmacy will not provide patients' personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With the patient's consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property
  • When particularly necessary for improving public health

Patient Rights

Patients have the following rights regarding their personal information:

  • Request for Disclosure: You may request disclosure of your personal information held by us
  • Correction/Deletion: If your information is inaccurate, you may request correction or deletion
  • Suspension of Use: You may request suspension of the use of your personal information

Please contact us at the pharmacy counter to make such requests.

Contact

For inquiries about personal information,
please contact us at the pharmacy counter.
